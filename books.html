The Famous Five

The Famous Five is one of the most popular children's book series by Enid Blyton. The story is about four children and their dog who go on exciting adventures during their holidays. They solve mysteries, explore secret places, and help others. The books teach friendship, courage, and teamwork.

The Secret Seven

The Secret Seven is a popular children's book series by Enid Blyton. It is about seven children who form a secret club. They solve mysteries, find clues, and help people. The stories focus on teamwork, friendship, and problem-solving.

Price: Rs. 1300

The MAgic Faraway Tree

he Magic Faraway Tree is a famous children's book by Enid Blyton. It tells the story of a magical tree where different lands come to the top, such as the Land of Take-What-You-Want and the Land of Goodies. Children visit these lands and experience exciting and magical adventures. The story teaches imagination, friendship, and kindness.
